What We Do & Why We Do

Shri Krishna Kripa Dham appears to be a temple dedicated to Lord Krishna, a prominent deity in Hinduism known for his teachings in the Bhagavad Gita and his divine acts as depicted in Hindu scriptures like the Mahabharata and the Bhagavata Purana. Temples dedicated to Lord Krishna are often places of devotion, prayer, and worship for followers of Hinduism, where they can engage in various rituals, ceremonies, and spiritual practices to deepen their connection with the divine.

Devotees often visit such temples to express their bhakti (devotion) towards Lord Krishna through acts such as offering prayers, performing aarti (rituals involving light), singing bhajans (devotional songs), and participating in religious festivals and celebrations dedicated to the deity.

If you have specific questions about Shri Krishna Kripa Dham or about the principles of bhakti and the concept of God in Hinduism, feel free to ask!
